Moving around Boston

Now there are a few ways you can move around Boston:

  1. You can just walk, if you want to put in those steps. Boston is a fairly walkable city, so put on some sneakers.
  2. Buy a bus, or subway ticket at the MBTA website, or buy at any of the train stations in the city.
